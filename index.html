Zork0

A boy that recently has changed of city because of the father's job. It was his first day in the city and he got lost. The "map" of the game where the boy will be moving is the new city where he is going to live. He will try to find his house. There will be a lot of problems that the boy have to find the solucion. Finally if he finds his house the game will finish.

This game is inspired in the game Zork, link: http://textadventures.co.uk/games/view/5zyoqrsugeopel3ffhz_vq/zork
